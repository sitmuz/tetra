Our Package

Basel

A city on the Rhine River in northern Switzerland near France and Germany. Often referred to as the Cultural Capital of the country.

Geneva

A city full of history, diplomacy, and natural beauty. Explore Lake Geneva, one of Europe’s oldest Old Towns, and see the headquarters of the UN.

Bern

The capital city of Switzerland. Full of medieval architecture, fountains, gardens, museums, and unique landmarks.

Zurich

Switzerland’s largest city. It’s great for shopping, museum-hopping, and taking day trips out into small villages and nature.

Interlaken

A holiday resort town near the Alps. The perfect place to base yourself for adventure activities and skiing/snowboarding in the Alps.

Lucerne

A beautiful, peaceful, historic town on a lake. Lucerne is known for music, art, museums, and its Old Town.
